I'm a Data Scientist at [Verasight](https://www.verasight.io) in San Francisco and a PhD candidate in Political Science at [UC Santa Barbara](https://www.polsci.ucsb.edu/people/ingmar-sturm).

At Verasight, I build ML pipelines for user segmentation, develop predictive models and attribution dashboards, and support a 230K+ member survey panel generating millions of responses. Previously, I worked as a researcher at the [Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research](https://www.pik-potsdam.de/en), as CTO at the Island Ark Project Foundation, and as a data strategy consultant at 180 Degrees Consulting.

My academic research examines how climate adaptation programs shape individual behavior and political attitudes in Indonesia. I use experimental and quasi-experimental methods with large-scale survey data (45K+ respondents across 30+ countries). My work has been published in *PNAS* and *International Affairs*, and has been funded by Meta, the Russel Sage Foundation, and UC Santa Barbara.
